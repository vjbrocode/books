Why we started

A thank-you letter that changed everything

Last year, my wife Tripti Kumari and I published our very first children's book. We simply wanted to create something meaningful for children — something that would encourage kindness, good habits, confidence, and a love for learning.

We never imagined we'd one day receive a thank-you letter from Save the Children Germany because of it. When it arrived, one sentence stayed with us:

Imagine never discovering the world of stories because there simply aren't any books to read.

For many of us, books were always part of childhood. For millions of children around the world, they're still a luxury. Save the Children brings books to children in remote communities — sometimes even transporting them on horseback — so every child gets the chance to read, learn, and dream.

That reminded us why we started writing children's books in the first place. So we made a promise: 100% of the income from every children's book we publish will be donated to Save the Children.

If you're buying a book for your own child — or looking for a meaningful gift for a niece, nephew, grandchild, or a friend's child — we'd be honored if you chose one of ours. This is only the beginning. We're already working on more books, with the second one releasing this year.
